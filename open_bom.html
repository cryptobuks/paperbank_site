Open BOM (Bill of Materials)

You need a Raspberry PI and a thermal printer

Plus you should find these things easily:

  • hdmi monitor (you can use any desktop computer's monitor or tv)
  • hdmi video cable
  • usb cable: usb <-> micro usb (type B)
  • usb charger (most cellphones charger are ok)
